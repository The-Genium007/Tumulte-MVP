# First Campaign Tutorial

This guide walks you through creating your first campaign in Tumulte.

## Prerequisites

- Tumulte is running (see [Installation](installation.md))
- You have a Twitch account

## Step 1: Login with Twitch

1. Open Tumulte in your browser
2. Click "Login with Twitch"
3. Authorize the application on Twitch
4. You'll be redirected back to Tumulte

## Step 2: Choose Your Role

After logging in, you'll be asked to choose your role:

- **Game Master (GM)**: Create campaigns and run polls
- **Streamer**: Participate in campaigns and display polls on your stream

For this tutorial, select **Game Master**.

## Step 3: Create a Campaign

1. Navigate to the Campaigns page
2. Click "Create Campaign"
3. Enter a name for your campaign (e.g., "The Dragon's Lair Adventure")
4. Add an optional description
5. Click "Create"

## Step 4: Invite Streamers

To run polls across multiple channels, invite streamers:

1. Open your campaign
2. Click "Invite Streamer"
3. Enter the streamer's Twitch username
4. The streamer will receive an invitation

## Step 5: Create a Poll Session

Polls are organized into sessions:

1. In your campaign, click "New Session"
2. Give the session a name (e.g., "Session 1 - The Tavern")
3. Click "Create"

## Step 6: Create Polls

Within a session, create polls for your viewers:

1. Click "Add Poll"
2. Enter your question: "What should the party do?"
3. Add choices:
   - Attack the dragon
   - Negotiate for peace
   - Flee through the back door
4. Set the duration (e.g., 60 seconds)
5. Save the poll

## Step 7: Run the Session

When you're ready to start:

1. Click "Start Session"
2. Invited streamers will be notified
3. Launch polls during your game to collect viewer votes

## For Streamers: Adding the Overlay

Streamers can display polls on their stream:

1. Accept the campaign invitation
2. Go to your overlay settings
3. Copy the overlay URL
4. Add it as a Browser Source in OBS
